Covalent bond: In covalent bonding atoms share electrons in order to achieve a stable configuration. This is the most common between non-metal atoms. Covalent bonding can take on a variety of forms including single, double, and triple bonds. For examples: in hydrogen molecule (\(H_2\)), water molecule (\(H_2O\)), methan gas molecule (\(CH_4\)), diamond (\(C_4\)), etc. Covalent bonding 1

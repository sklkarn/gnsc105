It is the quantity of motion contained in a body. If a body of mass \(m\) is moving with a velocity \(v\) then its momentum, \(p =mv\text{.}\) Momentum (also called linear Momentum) is a product of mass and velocity of the object. Momentum is represented by \(p\text{,}\) its SI unit is \(kgm/s\text{.}\) Momentum is a vector quantity and its direction is along the velocity of the object.

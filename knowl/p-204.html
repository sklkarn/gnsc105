Work: an object is displaced along the same direction as of the applied force then work is said to be done by the force. In the FigureÂ 3.1.1 force \(F\) is pushing a block of mass \(m\) along its direction for a distance of \(d\text{,}\) then work done, \(W\) by this force can be calculated by

Work is a scalar quantity as it is a measure of transfer of energy from one object to another. The SI unit of work is Joule\(=N\cdot m\text{.}\) In FPS system, \(ft.lb = foot.pound\text{,}\) and in CGS system, \(erg =dyne.cm\) are the work units.

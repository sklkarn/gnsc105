Rainbow is formed when sunlight is dispersed by rain droplets which are suspended in air. When sunlight enters the raindrop it refracts then it undergoes dispersion and total internal reflection from the rear end of the droplet surface and again refracted out in the atmosphere and that's how we see a rainbow.

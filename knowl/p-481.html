Laws of Reflection:
  1. The first law of reflection states that the incident ray, the reflected ray, and the normal to the point of incident all lie in the same plane.
  2. The second law of reflection states that the angle of reflection is equal to the angle of incidence.
Consider a light ray, AO is incident on a smooth surface, like morror at an angle \(\theta_i\) and bounces off, then from \(2^{nd}\) law of reflection
\begin{equation} \theta_i = \theta_r\tag{6.2.1} \end{equation}

    1. \(v_{f}=v_{i}+at\text{.}\)

  2. Equations of motion in vertical direction: Section 2.3
    1. \(v_{f}=v_{i}+gt\text{.}\)
    2. \(v^2_{f}=v^2_{i}+2gh\text{.}\)
    3. \(h=v_{i}t+\frac{1}{2}gt^2\text{.}\)
Here, \(g\) = acceleration due to gracvity, \(h\) = height attains by the object, and \(t\) = time taken by the object during its motion.

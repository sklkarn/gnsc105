Speed:The rate of change of position of an object is called a speed. It states that how fast the object is changing its position with respedt to time. In physics, the rate means with respect to time. It is also a scalar quantity. speed is represented by the letter, \(v\text{.}\) If \(x_{i}\) is an initial position and \(x_{f}\) is the final position of the object then
\begin{equation} v=\frac{x_f-x_i}{t_f-t_i} = \frac{d}{t}\text{.}\tag{2.1.0.1} \end{equation}
It's unit is m/s in SI system and cm/s in CGS system, and ft/s in FPS system.

Wave speed, \(v\) is telling how fast the information propogates. Suppose wave covers (information reaches) a distance \(\lambda\) in time \(T\text{,}\) then from equation (2.1), we have
\begin{equation*} v=\frac{\lambda}{T} \end{equation*}
Therefore, using equation (6.1)
\begin{equation} v = f \times \lambda\tag{6.2} \end{equation}

A hurricane is a tropical cyclone that occurs in the Atlantic Ocean and northeastern Pacific Ocean. It is a rotating storm system that produces strong winds, heavy rain, storm surges, and flooding. Hurricanes are classified according to their wind speed such as catagory one through five. The higher the category, the more powerful the hurricane and the more damage it can cause. Hurricanes can be extremely dangerous and have the potential to cause significant damage to property, infrastructure, and human life. It is important to be prepared and heed the warnings and advice of local authorities during hurricane season.

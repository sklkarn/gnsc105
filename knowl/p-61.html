There are three equations of motion defined by the above terminologies which can help solve problems of motion in horizontal direction. These eqations are obtained by manipulating of average velocity and acceleration of the object. They are as follows:
  1. \(v_{f}=v_{i}+at\text{.}\)
  2. \(v^2_{f}=v^2_{i}+2ad\text{.}\)
  3. \(d=v_{i}t+\frac{1}{2}at^2\text{.}\)
